About

StudioPaw88 sits between street documentation and symbolic engineering.

Cities don’t speak — they reveal themselves through symbols. We collect those symbols, archive them, and remix them into tools, writing, and design systems.

The work leans cyberpunk: surveillance aesthetics, authority signage, redaction, thresholds, and the quiet theatre of compliance.

  • Based: Sydney / online
  • Focus: street capture, zines, web builds
  • Available for: small collabs + commissions

Operating principles

  • Capture the signal, not the noise.
  • Design like it’s evidence.
  • Make systems that hold under pressure.
  • Leave an easter egg for the attentive.
